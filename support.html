❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I identify a plant?

Simply open the app, take a photo of the plant, and let the AI identify it instantly.

Why is my plant not being identified correctly?

Make sure the photo is clear, well-lit, and focused on the plant. Try capturing different angles if needed.

Is the app really free?

Yes, the app is currently 100% free with no hidden charges.

Are my images stored?

No, images are used only for processing and are not stored permanently.
